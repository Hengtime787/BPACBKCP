Dirk Visser

Innovative • Dedicated • Inspirational

We had the opportunity to interview Dirk Visser, founder of Allegiance Health Benefits. We asked him questions about his journey, and how he contributed to the incredible rise of the Missoula, MT Community.

Background

Dirk Visser is a now retired Entreprenuer from Missoula MT. He started the company Allegiance Benefit Plan Mangement, which specializes in health benefit plans for companies. As it grew, Allegiance supported more and more local Missoula businesses, which Visser calls "The heart and soul of the Missoula Community."
