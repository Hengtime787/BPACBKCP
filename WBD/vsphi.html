Philanthropy

  • Dirk Visser and his wife have been very active in the local non-profit community giving their time to host fundraisers and other events.

  • Allegiance contributed to many non-profit organisations and projects, such as Allegiance Field, a local Missoula baseball stadium.

  • Allegiance also helped local businesses thrive, through their regular service, which solves many problems for employers, and employees.

Business Givings

Visser states, "What we found was a niche that we felt we could be helpful and provide a needed service for, which we ended up doing." He went on to explain that if a business doesn't provide value to the local commuunity, it's not going to become popular. The business has to be competitive, and genuinly solve a problem in the community. He said, "...Everything's relative to what you're comparing it to." Just by getting sales, local businesses already contribute to the local economy, helping it stay strong.

His Advice

We asked Dirk Visser for some advice for younger people about how to give back to their communities. He said, "You have to find things that you're passionate about, and interested in. And you might not know that right off the top. I would encourage kids to get involved with various different things that are going on at the time."
