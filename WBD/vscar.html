Career

  • Visser's first job in the health insurance industry was an internship with a health benefits company.

  • While he was there, he learned a lot about the industry, and was intrigued by how it could be better.

  • He saw many things that could be improved in the industry, especially with the rapidly developing IT capabilities.

  • Then, using what he picked up, he decided to leave, and create his own company, which is now known as Allegiance Health Benefits.

  • He had no idea that it would grow into a company of over 500 employees, and help countless local businesses and nonprofits around Montana.

Growth

Allegiance now has many more employees than the two that it started with. But how did they get here? Well, their rapid growth was caused by a few things. Innovative computerized business model, local partnership, and the fact that the industry was quit vacant, giving Allegiance a decent amount of the market when it was started.

Computerization

Allegiance was started in the era of rapid growth in the IT industry. And so, taking advantage of this, Allegiance used computerized systems for their clients. Dirk Visser was really on of the Pioneers of the industry, introducing revoloutionary concepts that were possible through tech.
